This personal creed is inspired from a quote by Reki Kawahara, "I'd rather trust and regret than doubt and regret".


I believed that regret and doubt could have been the lowest points in one's life. This is not what I intend to put in some thought, especially during the pandemic.


Now, this is the question that I asked myself: "Is there an exact definition of such moment?" To rephrase, the question implies that not everything is what it seems. Just cherish and be positive and happy to such moment.
